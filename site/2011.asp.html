Bloomsday Race History

2011

Winners: Simon Ndirangu Misiker Mekonnin
Registered: 56,652
Finished: 51,303
Temperatures:
  1. Start: 37
  2. Middle: 49
  3. End: 55
Wind speed and direction: SW 8
Hours of sunshine: 12.5
Cool and moist days in late April and clear skies early on Bloomsday made for some lowland and river bottom fog before start time. This all dissipated by 9: 00 a.m and clear skies and light winds prevailed over the course through midday. Some fair weather clouds developed after noon but skies remained mostly clear with light winds. The high temperature for the day was a cool 58 degrees.
